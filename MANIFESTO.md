# The MiLyfe Brain Manifesto

## Digital Sovereignty for AI

We believe in a future where AI works **for** you — not for a corporation.

---

## Our Principles

### 1. Your Data Stays Yours

Your code never leaves your machine. Not for training. Not for analytics. Not for "improving our services." When you run MiLyfe Brain, every byte of your intellectual property remains on hardware you own and control.

This is not a privacy policy. It is an architectural guarantee.

### 2. Your Compute Is Your Own

You are not renting intelligence by the hour. You are not paying per token. You are not subject to rate limits imposed by someone else's business model. Your GPU, your CPU, your electricity — your AI.

### 3. No Lock-In, Ever

Every piece of data MiLyfe Brain creates is in open formats:
- Playbooks: JSON
- Memory: SQLite + ChromaDB
- Config: YAML
- Exports: Standard formats (GitHub Actions, Makefile)

You can leave at any time and take everything with you. We compete on quality, not captivity.

### 4. Transparent Reasoning

Every decision an agent makes is auditable. Every tool call is logged. Every thought is visible. There are no black boxes in MiLyfe Brain. When an agent writes code, you can trace exactly why it made every choice.

### 5. Human Sovereignty

MiLyfe Brain asks before it acts. It never overrides human judgment on destructive operations. The approval system exists not because we don't trust the AI, but because we respect the human. You are the authority. Always.

### 6. Open Source as a Right

The source code is MIT-licensed. You can read it, modify it, redistribute it, sell it, or use it to build something better. We believe that infrastructure this important should be owned by everyone.

### 7. Accessible to All

No API keys. No credit cards. No sign-ups. No waitlists. No enterprise tiers that gate critical features. If you have a computer, you can run MiLyfe Brain. The democratization of AI is not a marketing slogan — it is the entire point.

---

## What We Oppose

- **Surveillance capitalism applied to development tools** — Your code patterns are not a product to be sold.
- **Artificial scarcity of intelligence** — Rate limits exist to maximize revenue, not to protect users.
- **Cloud dependency as a business model** — Your productivity should not depend on someone else's uptime.
- **Training on user data without consent** — Every "free tier" has a hidden cost.
- **Planned obsolescence of local tools** — Software that stops working when a server shuts down is not software you own.

---

## Our Commitment

MiLyfe Brain will always be:

- **Free** — No paid tiers, no premium features, no artificial limitations
- **Local** — Zero cloud services required for full functionality
- **Private** — Zero telemetry, zero phone-home, zero data collection
- **Open** — MIT license, public development, community governance
- **Portable** — Standard formats, easy export, no lock-in

---

## The Future We're Building

We envision a world where:

Every developer has a personal AI swarm that knows their codebase, understands their preferences, and gets better over time — running entirely on their own hardware, answering to no one but them.

This is not a product vision. It is a statement of values.

**Your intelligence. Your machine. Your rules.**
