# The MiLyfe Brain Manifesto

## Digital Sovereignty for the AI Age

We believe that artificial intelligence should amplify human agency, not extract it. MiLyfe Brain exists because the dominant model — where your thoughts, data, and compute flow through corporate servers — is not inevitable. It is a choice. We choose differently.

---

## Our 7 Principles

### 1. Your Data Stays Yours
Every conversation, memory, document, and generated artifact lives on your hardware. Nothing leaves your machine. No telemetry, no analytics, no "anonymous" usage data. Your intellectual work belongs to you — period.

### 2. Your Compute Is Yours
AI inference runs on your CPU/GPU through Ollama. You own the silicon, you own the cycles, you own the results. No metered API calls, no throttled access, no monthly bills that scale with your creativity.

### 3. No Lock-In, Ever
Standard formats. Open protocols. Export everything at any time. Your playbooks are JSON. Your database is SQLite. Your vector store speaks REST. Walk away whenever you want and take everything with you.

### 4. Transparent Reasoning
Every agent thought is visible. Every tool call is logged. Every decision has an audit trail. You can inspect, replay, and understand exactly what the system did and why. No black boxes.

### 5. Human Sovereignty
AI proposes, humans dispose. Destructive actions require explicit approval. The human-in-the-loop is not a bottleneck — it is a feature. You remain the authority over your own system.

### 6. Open Source as a Right
The full source code is available under MIT. Read it, modify it, redistribute it. Security through obscurity is not security. Auditability is a prerequisite for trust.

### 7. Accessible to All
No cloud subscriptions. No enterprise tiers. No "contact sales." If you have a computer that can run Docker and Ollama, you have access to the full platform. Economic barriers to AI tools are artificial — we reject them.

---

## What We Oppose

- **Surveillance capitalism** disguised as "AI assistants" that monetize your prompts and conversations to train proprietary models
- **Artificial scarcity** of compute through API rate limits, usage-based pricing, and tiered access that gates capability behind recurring payment
- **Data colonialism** where your creative output, personal context, and intellectual labor trains models you never consented to improve
- **Vendor lock-in** through proprietary formats, walled-garden ecosystems, and astronomical switching costs designed to trap users
- **Opacity** in systems that make consequential decisions without explanation, audit trail, or mechanism for user override
- **Centralized control** where a single company can deprecate features, change terms, or revoke access at will without recourse

---

## Our Commitment

MiLyfe Brain will **never**:
- Phone home, collect telemetry, or track usage
- Require an internet connection for core functionality
- Gate features behind payment or registration
- Sell, share, or train on user data
- Introduce closed-source components into the core platform
- Require account creation or authentication for local use
- Degrade functionality to upsell a paid tier

MiLyfe Brain will **always**:
- Run fully offline after initial setup
- Remain 100% open source under the MIT license
- Prioritize user control over platform convenience
- Document its architecture, decisions, and trade-offs transparently
- Welcome community contributions without CLA requirements
- Support commodity hardware — no enterprise GPU required
- Provide escape hatches: export everything, migrate anywhere

---

## The Future We're Building

We envision a world where personal AI is as fundamental and private as personal computing. Where running an intelligent assistant is no more remarkable than running a web browser — local, fast, yours. MiLyfe Brain is one step toward that future.

The tools exist. The models are open. The only question is whether we build the infrastructure to keep them in the hands of individuals rather than institutions.

We choose individuals. Every time.
